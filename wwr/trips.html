Get to know our three levels of rafting!

>>> Ideal for beginners or families, this relaxing trip takes you down a calm river surrounded by stunning mountain views. You'll receive basic paddling and safety instructions, with stops for rest and photos. Perfect for those looking for a peaceful rafting experience.

Location: Mendoza River, Argentina // Duration: 3 hours // Difficulty: Easy (Class I - II)

ITINERARY:

  • 09:00 AM - Meet at the rafting base, registration, and gear distribution (lifejacket, helmet, paddle).
  • 09:30 AM - Introduction to basic paddling techniques and water safety.
  • 10:00 AM - Start the descent on the river, enjoying the mountain landscapes and mild currents.
  • 11:30 AM - Stop at a natural beach to rest and take photos.
  • 12:00 PM - End of the trip, return to the base and farewell.

>>> This trip offers moderate adrenaline as you navigate Class III and IV rapids in a tropical rainforest setting. With a waterfall stop for swimming and a riverside lunch, it's perfect for those seeking adventure without extreme difficulty.

Location: Pacuare River, Costa Rica //Duration: 6 hours // Difficulty: Medium (Class III - IV)

ITINERARY:

  • 07:00 AM - Meet at the meeting point, breakfast, and safety briefing.
  • 08:00 AM - Transfer to the starting point on the river.
  • 09:00 AM - Begin the descent, tackling exciting rapids surrounded by tropical rainforest.
  • 11:30 AM - Stop at a waterfall to swim and take photos.
  • 12:30 PM - Lunch by the riverbank with a view of the rainforest.
  • 02:00 PM - Second part of the descent with more intense rapids.
  • 03:30 PM - Arrival at the final point, return transfer to the hotel.

>>> A challenging, multi-day journey through Class IV and V rapids in the Grand Canyon. This intense trip includes hiking, camping, and navigating some of the most difficult rapids, ideal for experienced adventurers looking for a rugged, remote experience.

Location: Grand Canyon, USA (Colorado River) // Duration: 3 days / 2 nights // Difficulty: High (Class IV - V)

ITINERARY:

Day 1:

  • 06:00 AM - Meet at the base and gear up.
  • 08:00 AM - Start the descent, navigating intense rapids.
  • 12:00 PM - Lunch at a river beach.
  • 03:00 PM - Explore canyons and go hiking.
  • 06:00 PM - Dinner and camping under the stars.

Day 2:

  • 07:00 AM - Breakfast and continue the descent.
  • 10:00 AM - Section with Class V rapids, the most challenging part.
  • 02:00 PM - Stop at a natural cave.
  • 06:00 PM - Dinner and campfire on the second night.

Day 3:

  • 07:00 AM - Final part of the journey with more technical rapids.
  • 12:00 PM - Arrival at the final point and return transfer.
